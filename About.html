Our Story

Mom's of Merced was founded in Merced CA during the Covid-19 Pandemic. A single mom who was experiencing motherhood for the first time during this global pandemic was struggling to navigate parenthood and decided to create a support group. Other moms followed, bringing their advice, questions, and little ones. We are always open for new adventures and suggestions!
